Hi there, I'm Kwame!

Thanks for checking out my site! I'm a recent graduate from the University of California, Davis where I completed my degree in economics. I am fascinated by the world around me, and aim to learn more about it in order to better understand problems we face in society today. As an aspiring young professional, I hope to pursue opportunities relating to finance and accounting where I can make a difference.

Education

Professional Experience

Student Financial Manager

University of California, Davis Business Center

At the UC Davis Business Center, my primary task was to compile sales information into a general ledger account data format. I then generated reports on these data points using online accounting software, and presented my findings to campus organizations. Other tasks included overseeing unit security for the handling of cash, as well as providing administrative support to the management by hiring and training 10+ employees.

Data Analyst Intern

JWOMPA Media Group

At JWOMPA, I researched data on music industry trends based on artists' number of sales as well as streams. I scraped the web for infromation relating to this, and made sense of the data using Excel spreadsheets to create data visuals such as graphs and dashboards. These findings were then used to record trends in music, and present media to customers based on their preference.

Undergraduate Research Assistant

University of California, Davis Business - Economics Department

As an undergraduate research assistant for the UC Davis Economics Department, I researched the effects of The Great Flood on California agriculture and farming industries in the 1800s. I prepared weekly presenations based on my findings and analysis, and presented these to my peers and research lead.

Certified Passport Agent

University of California, Davis Business Center

During my first role at the UC Davis Business Center, my job was to prepare and track passport forms for all applicants. I learned the ins and outs of a professional organization and their workflow, as well as the importance of proper accounting in cross-functional organizations where payments and sensitive information are being handled.
